Refer the below points to know how to play:
  • Please signup/login with your email.
  • Tap on the tree which is present on the home screen. You can see your taps increasing in the above taps bar.
  • You can tap 100,000 times per day. (Limit - 100,000 taps)
  • Once you complete 1,000,000 taps you can go to the “Redeem” section and “Redeem” the Giftcard you want.
  • You will receive your Giftcard in 15 days to your registered email.

    Note: If you redeemed Solana you'll receive crypto to the address you provide make sure to double check

  • If any suspicious activity is detected in the account your account will be banned without any further questioning.
